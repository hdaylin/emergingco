Methods: the civil works differentiator

The majority of projects undertaken by the Civil and Infrastructure division are larger than life: huge, challenging and unique. For this reason, Pomerleau created a dedicated, in-house methods team. This group analyzes the construction phases and schedule; determines equipment needs as well as the design and manufacture of special tools; and develops and plans work flows. It also serves as the link between the construction and logistics teams and the estimation department. A methods service focused on each project from estimation through to execution translates into saving client's money and time, respecting schedules and ensuring safer worksites.

Own-Forces Work

The Civil and Infrastructure team can take on your project with its own forces using experienced staff, cutting-edge equipment and custom methods. Areas of expertise include piling works, civil and earthworks, formwork and pipelines. Motivated and effective teams direct and deliver your projects while fully respecting quality and safety objectives.
