ACQ awards regional trophy to Pomerleau

At its annual conference from September 30 to October 1, 2016, the Quebec Construction Association (ACQ) celebrated excellence by honouring 24 successful commercial and residential construction businesses.

The Association presented the "Construire 2016" regional trophy for the Quebec City region to Pomerleau, noting that the Pomerleau team excels in design-build and is on the leading edge of the latest technological trends.

Indeed, at the conference, Pomerleau's BIM/VDC Director, Ivanka Iordanova, was the keynote speaker and addressed the topic "New technologies serving the construction industry," and the Pomerleau booth showcased the use of virtual reality in its projects.
